# Character Design & Scene Breakdown
## Song: Pages of Us
## Artist: Suno AI
## Selected Story: The Last Run

---

## VISUAL ATMOSPHERE BLOCK

Contemporary urban noir set during a single cold winter night (10 PM to 4 AM), creating stark visual contrast between two worlds: the streets rendered in harsh cold blue streetlights with deep shadows, neon signs casting cyan and magenta glows, visible breath in freezing air, steam rising from manholes creating atmospheric haze, wet asphalt reflecting fragmented light like broken mirrors; versus domestic spaces bathed in warm amber and soft golden tones, intimate tungsten lighting creating safe havens of warmth, soft fabric textures and lived-in comfort. Handheld camera aesthetic for street scenes suggesting tension and instability, locked-off stable framing for home scenes suggesting safety and intimacy. Color temperature progression from cold (2800K blue) to warm (3200K amber) as protagonist moves toward his choice. Textures emphasize urban grit (concrete, metal, glass), fabric softness (hoodies, bedding), and the contrast between hard streets and soft domesticity. Cinematic realism inspired by *Moonlight* meets *City of God*—raw, authentic, emotionally vulnerable, not glamorizing street life but showing its psychological cost. Shallow depth of field isolating characters in their emotional states, atmospheric lighting creating mood over clarity, intimate framing emphasizing internal conflict and human connection over spectacle.

## CHARACTER DESCRIPTIONS

### Character 1: Marcus (Protagonist)

**Physical Appearance:**
- Age: 26
- Height/Build: 6'0", athletic build with broad shoulders, lean muscular physique suggesting street awareness and physical capability
- Skin Tone: Deep brown skin with warm undertones
- Hair: Short fade haircut, clean lines, well-maintained, natural black hair texture
- Eyes: Dark brown eyes, expressive and intense, carrying visible exhaustion and internal conflict
- Distinctive Features: Strong jawline, full lips, slight stubble (2-3 days growth), defined cheekbones, intense gaze that shifts between hardened street awareness and vulnerable emotion
- Overall Look: A young man caught between two identities—street-hardened exterior with moments of deep vulnerability breaking through. His face tells the story of someone who's seen too much but hasn't lost his humanity.

**Costume/Styling:**
- Primary Outfit: Black heavyweight hoodie (consistent across all scenes)
  - Oversized fit, slightly worn but clean
  - Hood down in most scenes (up in street scenes for anonymity)
  - Front kangaroo pocket where hands often rest
  - Dark charcoal or black color, non-branded, street-authentic
  - Underneath: simple dark gray or black t-shirt (visible when hoodie is unzipped)
- Bottoms: Dark blue/black jeans, slim fit, slightly worn
- Footwear: Black high-top sneakers (Nike or similar street-authentic style)
- Accessories: Thin gold chain (subtle, not ostentatious), visible at neckline; smartphone in hand or pocket (key prop)
- Additional: Dark jacket over hoodie in coldest street scenes (optional layer)

**Emotional States Required:**

1. **Marcus (Core - Internal Conflict)**
   - Sitting in car driver's seat, phone in hand illuminated by screen glow, face lit from below by phone creating dramatic shadows, eyes staring at phone screen with furrowed brow, jaw clenched, free hand gripping steering wheel, shoulders tense, posture suggesting paralysis between two choices, expression showing deep internal struggle—fear, doubt, and exhaustion all visible

2. **Marcus (Street Mode - Hardened)**
   - Standing on street corner or near car, hood up partially shadowing face, hands in hoodie pocket, posture alert and guarded, shoulders squared, chin slightly raised, eyes scanning environment with street awareness, expression closed-off and unreadable (street mask), body language suggesting controlled tension and readiness, gold chain barely visible at neckline

3. **Marcus (With Crew - False Confidence)**
   - Leaning against car or wall with crew members in background (shadowy figures), arms crossed or one hand dapping up crew member, slight smile or smirk that doesn't reach his eyes, posture relaxed but performative, trying to project confidence and belonging, but subtle tension visible in shoulders and eyes, the mask of street respect worn uncomfortably

4. **Marcus (Flashback - Younger/Authentic)**
   - Softer expression, genuine smile, eyes bright without the weight of street life, posture more open and relaxed, hoodie unzipped showing t-shirt underneath, no gold chain (simpler times), leaning toward Nia or sitting close to her, body language showing authentic connection and vulnerability, the man before the streets hardened him

5. **Marcus (The Choice - Determination)**
   - In car, phone in hand typing message, face illuminated by phone screen glow, expression shifting from conflict to resolution, jaw set with determination, eyes focused and clear (decision made), slight relief visible in relaxed shoulders, one hand on steering wheel ready to drive, posture shifting from paralysis to action

6. **Marcus (Homecoming - Vulnerable Relief)**
   - Standing in apartment doorway, hoodie still on but hood down, arms slightly open or at sides (non-defensive), face showing exhaustion mixed with relief, eyes soft and vulnerable (street mask completely dropped), slight emotional tremor in expression (holding back tears or just released them), shoulders dropped from released tension, body language completely open and unguarded

7. **Marcus (Embrace - Peace)**
   - Arms wrapped around Nia in tight embrace, face buried in her shoulder or hair, eyes closed, expression of complete surrender to emotion and relief, body curved protectively around her, all tension released, posture suggesting he's finally home, warm lighting on his face, the weight visibly lifted

**Character-Specific Notes:**
- The hoodie is THE defining visual element—it must appear identical in every scene (same wear patterns, same fit)
- Marcus's gold chain should be subtle and consistent—visible but not flashy
- His phone is a key prop that appears in multiple scenes (texting Nia, texting crew, the decisive message)
- Posture shift is critical: tense/guarded in street scenes → open/vulnerable in home scenes
- The transformation from "street Marcus" to "authentic Marcus" should be visible through body language, not costume change

### Character 2: Nia (Partner)

**Physical Appearance:**
- Age: 24
- Height/Build: 5'5", slender build with natural curves, graceful but not fragile
- Skin Tone: Medium brown skin with golden undertones
- Hair: Natural hair in medium-length braids (box braids or similar protective style), pulled back or over shoulder, dark brown/black color
- Eyes: Warm brown eyes, expressive and emotionally transparent, showing months of accumulated fear and exhaustion
- Distinctive Features: Soft facial features, full lips, high cheekbones, delicate hands, natural beauty without heavy styling
- Overall Look: A young woman worn down by constant fear but still holding onto love. Her beauty is natural and understated, her exhaustion visible but her strength evident. She looks like someone who's been waiting too long for someone to come home.

**Costume/Styling:**
- Primary Outfit: Comfortable home clothing (intimate, lived-in)
  - Oversized cream or light gray knit sweater (could be Marcus's old sweater suggesting intimacy)
  - Sleeves pushed up to elbows or pulled over hands for comfort
  - Soft, worn-in fabric suggesting comfort and domesticity
  - Underneath: simple tank top or t-shirt
- Bottoms: Soft gray or black leggings or comfortable joggers
- Footwear: Barefoot or simple house slippers (barely visible)
- Hair: Braids pulled back in low ponytail or draped over one shoulder, a few loose strands framing face
- Accessories: Minimal—small stud earrings, perhaps simple necklace; no makeup or very minimal (natural, at-home look)
- Additional: In some scenes, wrapped in soft blanket or throw

**Emotional States Required:**

1. **Nia (Core - Anxious Waiting)**
   - Sitting on edge of bed or couch, phone in hand checking messages, body leaning forward slightly in anticipation, shoulders tense, one hand holding phone while other hand fidgets or rests on lap, face showing worry and exhaustion, eyes focused on phone screen with furrowed brow, lips pressed together, posture suggesting she's been in this position for hours, warm home lighting creating intimate atmosphere

2. **Nia (Texting - Desperate Hope)**
   - Close-up or medium shot, phone held in both hands actively typing, face illuminated by phone screen glow in dim room, expression showing mixture of hope and fear as she composes message, eyes glistening with unshed tears, biting lower lip in concentration or anxiety, braids falling forward framing face, body hunched slightly over phone, the vulnerability of reaching out one more time

3. **Nia (Alone - Grief and Exhaustion)**
   - Lying in bed or curled on couch, wrapped in blanket or hugging pillow, face partially obscured by fabric or hair, eyes closed or staring blankly at nothing, expression of deep sadness and resignation, body in fetal position suggesting self-comfort and protection, the weight of months of fear visible in every line of her posture, warm but lonely lighting

4. **Nia (Flashback - Happy/In Love)**
   - Genuine bright smile, eyes lit with joy and laughter, head tilted back or leaning toward Marcus, body relaxed and open, sitting close to him on torn couch, wearing simpler clothing (suggesting earlier time), no worry lines on face, expression of pure happiness and authentic connection, the woman before fear became her constant companion

5. **Nia (Reading Message - Disbelief/Hope)**
   - Phone in hand, eyes wide reading Marcus's "I'm done. I'm coming home" message, mouth slightly open in surprise, one hand covering mouth or pressed to chest, expression shifting from disbelief to cautious hope, body frozen in moment of processing, tears beginning to form, the instant before relief floods in

6. **Nia (Door Opening - Overwhelming Relief)**
   - Standing near apartment door as it opens, arms beginning to reach toward Marcus, face crumpling with emotion (relief, joy, residual fear all at once), tears streaming down face, mouth open in sob or gasp, body moving forward into embrace, all the held tension releasing at once, warm doorway lighting creating halo effect

7. **Nia (Embrace - Finally Breathing)**
   - Arms wrapped tightly around Marcus, face pressed into his hoodie, eyes closed, tears on cheeks, expression of complete emotional release, body sagging into him (he's holding her up), hands clutching fabric of his hoodie, the physical manifestation of "finally can breathe," all fear released in this moment

**Character-Specific Notes:**
- Nia is always in home/domestic settings—she represents the warm, safe world Marcus is choosing
- Her oversized sweater should appear consistent across home scenes
- Her phone is also a key prop (texting Marcus, waiting for responses)
- The contrast between "flashback happy Nia" and "present exhausted Nia" should be striking
- Her braids should remain consistent in style across all scenes
- Emotional transparency is key—her face shows everything Marcus's street mask hides

## NOTES FOR ASSET GENERATION (Phase 3)

**Consistency Anchors:**
- Marcus's black hoodie must be identical across all scenes (same fit, same wear)
- Marcus's gold chain must be visible and consistent
- Nia's braids and oversized sweater must remain consistent
- Color temperature: Cold blue (streets) = 5500-6500K, Warm amber (home) = 2800-3200K

**Key Prop:**
- Phone screens will need specific text overlays in post-production for scenes 02, 05, 18

**Lighting Reference:**
- Street scenes: Harsh overhead streetlights, neon accent colors (cyan, magenta), deep shadows
- Home scenes: Soft tungsten practicals (lamps), warm ambient glow, intimate and safe

**Emotional Arc Visual Progression:**
- Scenes 1-8: Cold dominates (Marcus in streets, paralyzed)
- Scenes 9-15: Cold/warm split (Marcus vs. Nia, separation)
- Scenes 16-17: Conflict peak (competing visions)
- Scenes 18-22: Warm dominates (choice made, reunion, resolution)
